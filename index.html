Next Gen. Socializing

Amii is user to user socializing app that allows you to meet up with others and attend events together. Users create posts relating to certain categories and events, and offer to attend events with other users.

iOS Android Learn More

What's Amii?

With Amii, you can share experiences with others that have similar interests. Ever wanted to do something, but didn't want to do it alone? Now you don't have to! Just find the experience you're looking for, and book yourself an Amii.

It also works both ways. Want to offer your services? You can create a posting an start earning in under a minute.

We want you to post or find anything you can think of. You can view an art gallery with someone, go for a bike ride, or even attend a wedding! The opportunities, activities, and events are endless on Amii!
